Goals of Project:

The purpose of my research was to examine and investigate the following:

  • The process in which the A.I. is programmed and how its codes are written.
  • How such intricate software can be compared to the way in which human beings process and relay information.
  • How A.I. can assist in propelling mankind into the next age of innovation.
  • The possible ethical issues that might arise once A.I. becomes more intelligent: Should they continue to be viewed as objects and tools? Or should they be considered a type of life form with its their freedom and rights?
